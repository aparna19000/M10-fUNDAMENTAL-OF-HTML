Facts About Coding

Coding, also known as computer programming, is the process of giving a computer instructions in a language it understands to perform specific tasks. It is the foundation of all software, websites, and apps we use daily. Learning to code enhances problem-solving skills, logical thinking, and creativity.

Did you know that the first computer programmer was Ada Lovelace, daughter of the poet Lord Byron? She wrote the world's first machine algorithm for Charles Babbage's Analytical Engine in the mid-1800s. Her work laid the groundwork for modern computing!
